Causes
As a concerned citizen of the world and a resident of Nova Scotia, the wellfare of the local environment is very important to me. Ensuring the protection of the wild spaces and provincial park of this province is important to the helath of the local environment, and the Ecology Action Centre is a non-profit charity organization that works to support the preservation of Nova Scotia's wildlife and support local policies and practises focused on adovacay for ecologically important action to fight climate change and the degradation of provincial ecosystems.

The Ecology Action Centre has been helping promote various causes since 1971, focused on promoting alternative practises and provincial policies that focus on protecting the local environment. Ranging from being early promoters of recycling in Halifax back in the 70s to more recent efforts to ammend the Provincial Parks Act to better protect green spaces from future development projects.
